Initiative for DEsign in Agrifood Systems, between three INRAE departments (ACT, TRANSFORM, AgroEcoSystem) and the Research and Innovation Directory from AgroParisTech, is a scientific network (around 100 scientists with a core group of 20) dedicated to research on and for design to support agri-food systems transitions towards more sustainability.
