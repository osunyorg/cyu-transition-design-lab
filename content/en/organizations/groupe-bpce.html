With 103,000 employees, the Group serves 35 million customers worldwide - individuals, professionals, companies, institutional investors, and local authorities. In France, it operates in retail banking and insurance through its two major networks, Banque Populaire and Caisse d'Epargne, as well as Banque Palatine and Oney. Internationally, it develops its corporate and investment banking activities through Natixis Corporate & Investment Banking, and its asset and wealth management businesses through Natixis Investment Managers.
